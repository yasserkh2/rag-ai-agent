# Medical Billing and Denial Management

## Service Overview
COB Solution's Medical Billing and Denial Management service helps healthcare practices improve claim accuracy, accelerate reimbursement, and respond more effectively to denials or underpayments. The service combines front-end information review, claims workflow support, response analysis, and follow-up activities intended to strengthen revenue cycle performance.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Gathering and reviewing patient and provider information
- Verification of core details needed for clean claim processing
- Claim generation based on services performed and applicable codes
- Claim scrubbing to reduce submission errors
- Daily claim submission to support faster reimbursement cycles
- Posting of insurance responses on a shared platform or workflow system
- Analysis of payer responses to identify denials, delays, or recurring patterns
- Follow-up and collection work on denied or underpaid claims
- Payment posting and resolution support for outstanding balances
- Monthly reporting on claim status, payments, and key performance metrics

## Common Practice Scenarios
- A practice is seeing too many denials caused by missing or incorrect claim details.
- Reimbursement is slow because claims are not being reviewed or submitted consistently.
- Staff need better visibility into insurance responses and denial trends.
- The billing team wants more structured follow-up on unpaid or underpaid claims.
- Leadership needs reporting that shows claim performance and revenue cycle health.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and primary billing contact
- Specialty, payer mix, and claim volume
- Current billing software, EMR, or clearinghouse tools
- Typical denial categories or underpayment issues
- Existing process for claim creation, scrubbing, and payment posting
- Reporting expectations and key revenue cycle goals
- Urgency, staffing constraints, and preferred onboarding timeline

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
When users ask about medical billing and denial management, the chatbot should explain that COB Solution supports the full claim workflow from information review and claim generation through denial analysis, follow-up, payment posting, and reporting. If the user is considering the service, the chatbot should gather billing context and route the conversation toward scheduling or human follow-up.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate to a human when the user asks for coding advice, payer-specific billing strategy, case-specific denial resolution, compliance guarantees, or implementation details tied to a live revenue cycle workflow. Human review is also appropriate when the prospect needs customized reporting or immediate operational support.
