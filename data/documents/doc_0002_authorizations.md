# Authorizations

## Service Overview
COB Solution's Authorizations service helps healthcare providers manage insurance authorization workflows more efficiently so clinical teams can stay focused on patient care. The service is positioned around timely request submission, better coordination, and clearer visibility into approval status.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Patient information verification through secure chat
- Collection of service details through shared EMR access or provider input
- Timely submission of authorization requests
- Live chat communication for updates and coordination
- Follow-up on pending approvals to reduce avoidable delays
- Approval tracking through a platform or EMR workflow
- Daily recommendations intended to improve revenue and operational efficiency

## Common Practice Scenarios
- A practice is experiencing delays because authorization requests are not submitted quickly enough.
- Staff members need a more organized way to collect authorization details from providers.
- A clinic wants better visibility into pending approvals and follow-up activity.
- The team is trying to reduce administrative burden tied to insurance coordination.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and main contact information
- Specialty or service lines that frequently require authorization
- Payers involved
- Current workflow for collecting patient and service details
- EMR or platform used by the practice
- Common authorization delays, denials, or approval bottlenecks
- Expected volume and turnaround needs

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
When users ask about authorizations, the chatbot should explain that COB Solution helps verify information, collect required service details, submit requests on time, follow up on approvals, and keep teams updated through ongoing communication. If the user wants help getting started, the chatbot should gather intake details and offer scheduling or human follow-up.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate to a human when the user asks for payer-specific rules, case-specific authorization advice, compliance assurances, integration details, or operational recommendations tied to a live workflow. Escalation is also appropriate when a prospect wants a customized setup or immediate onboarding support.
