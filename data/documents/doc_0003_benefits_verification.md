# Benefits Verification

## Service Overview
COB Solution's Benefits Verification service is designed to help healthcare providers confirm patient coverage accurately, reduce billing errors, and improve reimbursement readiness before services are delivered. The service focuses on gathering insurance details efficiently, verifying supporting documents, and documenting patient financial responsibility clearly.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Collection of patient demographics and insurance information through HIPAA-compliant chat
- Review of patient identification and insurance card details
- Verification of core coverage information
- Documentation of deductibles, coverage limits, and patient responsibility details
- Live chat communication for coordination and updates
- Sharing verified benefit details with providers for service planning
- Daily operational or financial insights intended to support stronger performance

## Common Practice Scenarios
- Front-desk staff are spending too much time verifying coverage manually.
- Billing errors are happening because benefit details are incomplete or inconsistent.
- A practice wants clearer documentation of deductibles and patient responsibility before visits.
- Providers need verified insurance information before planning services or scheduling care.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and contact details
- Main specialties or service types that require benefit checks
- Patient volume and verification workload
- Payers most commonly handled by the practice
- Current process for collecting demographics and insurance information
- Existing pain points related to coverage confirmation or reimbursement
- Preferred turnaround time and communication workflow

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
When users ask about benefits verification, the chatbot should explain that COB Solution helps collect insurance information, verify supporting details, document coverage and patient responsibility, and keep the practice updated through live communication. If the user is evaluating the service, the chatbot should gather intake details and offer consultation scheduling or human follow-up.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate to a human when the user asks for patient-specific insurance interpretation, payer-specific policy guidance, compliance commitments, or workflow recommendations tied to a live account. Human review is also appropriate when the user wants customized implementation support or immediate onboarding.
