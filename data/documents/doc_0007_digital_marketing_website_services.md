# Digital Marketing and Website Services

## Service Overview
COB Solution's Digital Marketing and Website Services are designed to help healthcare providers and related businesses strengthen their online presence, attract the right audience, and convert interest into measurable business growth. The service emphasizes customized strategy, performance visibility, and marketing support that allows healthcare teams to stay focused on operations and patient care.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Targeted social media campaigns with ads and promotional content
- SEO optimization to improve search visibility and website traffic
- Audience-focused content creation
- Data-driven advertising strategy aimed at better return on investment
- Website traffic and conversion improvement support
- Performance analytics and regular reporting
- Customized strategies aligned to practice goals
- Reputation management support
- Email marketing campaigns for lead nurturing and engagement
- Graphic design support to strengthen brand identity

## Common Practice Scenarios
- A healthcare practice wants to improve visibility in a competitive local market.
- Leadership wants more patient leads from digital channels.
- The organization needs a better website and clearer conversion strategy.
- Marketing efforts are active but results are inconsistent or hard to measure.
- A clinic wants to improve its online reputation and brand presentation.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Organization name and marketing contact
- Website URL and current digital presence
- Specialty, target audience, and service area
- Primary goals such as lead generation, awareness, or conversion improvement
- Current marketing channels and campaign performance
- Branding, content, or reputation challenges
- Preferred reporting cadence, timeline, and budget expectations

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
When users ask about digital marketing and website services, the chatbot should explain that COB Solution helps improve visibility, engagement, lead generation, and brand presentation through tailored digital strategies. If the user wants to explore next steps, the chatbot should gather marketing context and route the conversation to consultation scheduling or human follow-up.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate to a human when the user asks for campaign strategy recommendations, pricing, creative scope, SEO guarantees, website implementation specifics, or growth planning tied to a live business situation. Human review is also appropriate when the user wants a customized package or immediate launch support.
