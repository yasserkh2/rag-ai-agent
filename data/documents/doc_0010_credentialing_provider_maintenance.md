# Credentialing and Provider Maintenance

## Service Overview
COB Solution's Credentialing and Provider Maintenance service helps healthcare providers complete enrollment, maintain accurate payer records, and stay current with ongoing credentialing requirements. The service is designed to reduce administrative friction so practices can focus on patient care while COB supports document collection, payer enrollment, profile setup, updates, and recredentialing workflows.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Collection of credentialing documents through HIPAA-compliant live chat
- Enrollment support for Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial insurers
- CAQH profile setup and maintenance assistance
- Application submission and follow-up support
- EFT, ERA, and eligibility-related setup support
- Ongoing updates to provider information such as licenses and NPI details
- Recredentialing support for existing payer relationships
- Contract support and related follow-up during the credentialing process
- Guidance through approval milestones so providers can begin billing with confidence

## Common Practice Scenarios
- A new provider needs to enroll with Medicare, Medicaid, or commercial payers.
- A practice is delayed because credentialing paperwork is incomplete or hard to track.
- Staff need help keeping provider records current across multiple payer systems.
- Recredentialing deadlines are approaching and the team needs support.
- A clinic wants smoother onboarding so providers can begin billing sooner.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and main credentialing contact
- Provider names, specialties, and locations
- Payers involved, including Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial insurers
- Current enrollment status for each provider
- Whether CAQH setup or maintenance is needed
- Known blockers such as missing documents, expired licenses, or pending applications
- Timeline, urgency, and target go-live date

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
When users ask about credentialing and provider maintenance, the chatbot should explain that COB Solution supports document collection, payer enrollment, CAQH setup, application follow-up, provider updates, and recredentialing. If the user wants to proceed, the chatbot should gather provider and payer details, then offer consultation scheduling or human follow-up.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate to a human when the user asks for payer-specific enrollment strategy, contract negotiation details, case-specific credentialing advice, compliance assurances, or help with a live stalled application. Human review is also appropriate when the practice needs a customized onboarding plan or immediate credentialing support.
