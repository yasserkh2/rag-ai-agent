# Medical Auditing

## Service Overview
COB Solution's Medical Auditing service helps healthcare practices, including physical therapy organizations, review documentation and billing accuracy to support compliance and stronger reimbursement performance. The service focuses on identifying documentation gaps, coding issues, and claim discrepancies that may affect revenue integrity or regulatory readiness.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Collection of billing records, claim details, and payment data
- Review of clinical documentation such as SOAP notes
- Evaluation of ICD-10 and CPT coding accuracy
- Claim review to identify undercoding, overcoding, or missing information
- Identification of errors that may affect revenue or compliance
- Actionable recommendations to improve documentation and operational accuracy
- Reporting that summarizes findings, risks, and financial improvement opportunities

## Common Practice Scenarios
- A practice wants to identify coding or documentation issues before they lead to denials or compliance concerns.
- Leadership needs a clearer view of revenue leakage caused by missing or inaccurate documentation.
- A clinic is preparing for growth and wants stronger internal auditing processes.
- Staff need guidance on recurring documentation and coding quality issues.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and primary audit contact
- Specialty, service lines, and documentation workflow
- Billing system, EMR, and coding process currently in use
- Types of records or claims the practice wants reviewed
- Recent denial patterns, compliance concerns, or audit triggers
- Whether the review is retrospective, ongoing, or tied to a specific operational issue
- Desired reporting depth and timeline

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
When users ask about medical auditing, the chatbot should explain that COB Solution reviews billing and documentation quality, identifies coding or compliance issues, and provides recommendations to improve financial and operational performance. If the user wants to explore the service, the chatbot should gather audit context and offer consultation scheduling or human follow-up.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate to a human when the user requests legal or regulatory interpretation, case-specific coding judgments, detailed compliance advice, or audit findings tied to live patient records. Human review is also appropriate when the user needs a customized audit scope or immediate operational support.
