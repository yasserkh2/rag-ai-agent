# Customer Care

## Service Overview
COB Solution's Customer Care service helps healthcare organizations improve patient communication, strengthen satisfaction, and support better retention through timely, compliant outreach. The service covers inquiry handling, appointment coordination, follow-up communication, re-engagement efforts, and feedback collection within a healthcare-focused communication workflow.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Handling patient inquiries through a HIPAA-compliant communication system
- Appointment scheduling and coordination support
- Reminder and follow-up outreach to improve engagement
- Re-engagement campaigns for lost or inactive patients through SMS and email
- Feedback collection and service quality improvement support
- Communication workflows designed to align with regulatory requirements
- Campaign management for compliant patient messaging

## Common Practice Scenarios
- A practice wants faster responses to patient questions and requests.
- Appointment attendance is inconsistent and reminder workflows need improvement.
- The organization wants to reconnect with inactive patients.
- Leadership needs a more structured way to gather and act on patient feedback.
- Staff want patient communication processes that are efficient and compliance-aware.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and primary contact information
- Communication channels currently in use
- Appointment workflow and scheduling process
- Patient engagement or retention challenges
- Need for reminders, reactivation campaigns, or feedback collection
- SMS and email communication preferences
- Compliance expectations and preferred response workflow

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
When users ask about customer care, the chatbot should explain that COB Solution supports patient communication through inquiry handling, scheduling support, reminders, re-engagement outreach, and feedback-driven improvement. If the user wants next steps, the chatbot should collect communication and workflow context, then offer consultation scheduling or human follow-up.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate to a human when the user asks for patient-specific case handling, regulatory interpretation, campaign strategy recommendations, system integration details, or custom workflow design. Human follow-up is also appropriate when the user is ready to onboard or needs a tailored communication program.
