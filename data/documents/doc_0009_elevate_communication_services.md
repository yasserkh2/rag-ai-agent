# Elevate Communication Services

## Service Overview
COB Solution's Elevate Communication Services are designed to improve communication between providers, patients, and other stakeholders through a mobile-friendly, internet-based business phone and messaging system. The service emphasizes accessibility, HIPAA-aware communication features, and a simpler alternative to traditional office phone setups.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Internet-based system setup with provider or office customization
- Desktop and mobile app access for calls and messages
- HD calling and advanced communication features
- Web-based and app-based faxing and secure messaging
- Voicemail-to-email workflows and call recording features
- HIPAA-compliant communication support
- Number porting for existing office phone numbers
- Fixed and predictable pricing compared with traditional carriers

## Common Practice Scenarios
- A healthcare office wants its business number to work across mobile and desktop devices.
- Staff need more flexible communication tools without adding extra hardware.
- The organization wants phone, fax, and messaging capabilities in one workflow.
- Leadership wants better communication reliability with predictable pricing.
- A practice is replacing or modernizing an older carrier-based phone setup.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Organization name and technical or operational contact
- Current phone and fax setup
- Number of users, providers, or office locations involved
- Need for mobile access, desktop calling, faxing, or voicemail workflows
- Existing phone number porting requirements
- Compliance expectations and recording or messaging needs
- Preferred implementation timeline

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
When users ask about Elevate Communication Services, the chatbot should explain that COB Solution offers an internet-based communication platform with mobile access, calling, messaging, faxing, voicemail support, and number porting. If the user wants next steps, the chatbot should gather office communication needs and route the conversation to scheduling or human follow-up.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate to a human when the user asks for technical implementation details, carrier migration planning, compliance guarantees, pricing specifics, integration questions, or a customized communication setup. Human follow-up is also appropriate when the user is ready to port numbers or begin deployment.
