# Financial Management

## Service Overview
Financial Management supports financial visibility, reporting discipline, and dashboard-based monitoring for practices that want a clearer view of operational performance. It is most helpful when leadership needs better reporting, cleaner tracking, or more confidence in how financial information is organized and reviewed.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Financial reporting support and review workflows
- Dashboard-oriented visibility into practice performance
- Monitoring processes for trends and exceptions
- Better organization of financial information for decision-making
- Follow-up on reporting gaps or unclear business signals

## Common Practice Scenarios
- Leadership lacks clear, timely financial visibility.
- Reports exist but are inconsistent or difficult to use.
- Teams want better dashboarding and monitoring of practice performance.
- Financial information is fragmented across people or systems.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and finance or leadership contact
- Current reporting process and tools
- Main visibility problems or unanswered business questions
- Key performance areas the practice wants to monitor
- Reporting frequency expectations
- Timeline and urgency

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
Start with a plain-language summary of the service, then clarify whether the user needs reporting support, dashboard visibility, or general financial workflow improvement. For follow-up, gather the reporting pain points that will help shape a more useful human conversation.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate when the user requests interpretation of sensitive financial data, asks for advice on a live financial decision, or needs recommendations tied to confidential records. Human review is better whenever the request requires business-specific analysis.

## Example Customer Questions
- What is included in financial management?
- Can you help if our reporting is inconsistent?
- What details should we prepare before a consultation?
- When should this type of request be escalated?
