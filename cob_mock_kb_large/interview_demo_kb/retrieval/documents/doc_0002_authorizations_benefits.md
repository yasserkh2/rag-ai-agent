# Authorizations and Benefits Verification

## Service Overview
Authorizations and Benefits Verification focuses on confirming coverage, validating benefits, checking referral requirements, and supporting prior authorization workflows before services are delivered or scheduled. The goal is to reduce avoidable denials and prevent visit delays caused by missing approval or incomplete insurance data.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Eligibility and active coverage checks
- Benefits verification for scheduled services
- Referral and prior authorization requirement review
- Status follow-up for pending authorizations
- Coordination to clarify payer requirements before care is delivered

## Common Practice Scenarios
- Staff members are spending too much time manually checking coverage.
- Visits or procedures are delayed because authorization requirements were missed.
- The practice wants cleaner pre-service workflows to reduce downstream denials.
- A team needs more consistency in how insurance and authorization steps are handled.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and contact details
- Payer names and service types involved
- Procedure or visit categories that need support
- Current authorization pain points or denial patterns
- Expected patient volume or scheduling pressure
- Preferred timeline and urgency

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
Give a direct explanation first, then clarify whether the user needs help with eligibility, benefits confirmation, or authorization workflow support. If the user is exploring implementation, collect payer and workflow context so the next step can be routed clearly.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate when the user requests case-specific insurance advice, shares repeated failures with a payer, or raises privacy or compliance concerns. Human review is also appropriate when the conversation shifts from general service information to a live authorization case.

## Example Customer Questions
- What is included in authorizations and benefits verification?
- Can this help reduce authorization-related delays?
- What should we prepare before discussing this service?
- When should an authorization issue be escalated to a human?
