# Credentialing and Provider Maintenance

## Service Overview
Credentialing and Provider Maintenance supports provider enrollment, payer applications, revalidation tracking, and ongoing updates to provider records. It is most useful when a practice needs reliable payer participation, faster onboarding for new clinicians, and fewer delays caused by incomplete or outdated provider data.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- New provider credentialing and payer enrollment support
- Revalidation tracking and renewal follow-up
- Demographic, taxonomy, and roster update coordination
- Provider record maintenance when licenses, locations, or affiliations change
- Issue review for stalled or rejected enrollment submissions

## Common Practice Scenarios
- A practice is onboarding a new physician and wants to reduce payer enrollment delays.
- A group has multiple providers with revalidation deadlines approaching.
- A billing team is seeing claim holds because provider records are outdated.
- A practice needs help keeping provider profiles aligned across payers and internal systems.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and primary contact details
- Provider names and specialties involved
- Payer list or target networks
- Enrollment status for each provider
- Known blockers such as missing documents or rejected submissions
- Requested timeline and urgency

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
Start with a concise explanation of the service, then ask whether the user needs new enrollment, maintenance, or revalidation support. If the user wants next steps, gather the intake details above and offer consultation scheduling or human follow-up.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate to a human when the user requests case-specific payer advice, reports repeated enrollment failures, or raises compliance, licensing, or privacy concerns. Escalation is also appropriate when the user needs document review tied to a live provider file.

## Example Customer Questions
- What is included in credentialing and provider maintenance?
- Can you help with payer enrollment for a new provider?
- What should we prepare before discussing credentialing support?
- When should a credentialing issue be escalated?
