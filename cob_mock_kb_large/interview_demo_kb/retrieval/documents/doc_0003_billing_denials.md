# Medical Billing and Denial Management

## Service Overview
Medical Billing and Denial Management supports claim submission quality, denial review, appeals follow-up, and revenue recovery efforts. It is designed for practices that want stronger clean-claim performance, better visibility into denial causes, and more consistent follow-through on underpaid or rejected claims.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Review of billing workflow gaps that create claim errors
- Denial categorization and trend analysis
- Appeals and follow-up support for unresolved claims
- Process recommendations to improve clean-claim rates
- Guidance on recurring payer-related pain points

## Common Practice Scenarios
- Claims are being denied for recurring reasons and the team needs better root-cause analysis.
- Appeals are piling up and follow-up is inconsistent.
- Revenue is slowing because billing workflows are fragmented or unclear.
- Leadership wants a more disciplined approach to denial prevention and recovery.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and billing contact details
- Top denial categories or payer concerns
- Current billing workflow summary
- Appeal backlog or unresolved claim patterns
- Reporting period or volume context
- Timeline, urgency, and desired business outcome

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
Answer at a high level first, then ask whether the user is focused on denial reduction, appeals support, or broader billing workflow improvement. For lead capture, gather the denial trends and payer context that will help a human team assess fit quickly.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate when the user asks for advice on a live payer dispute, shares sensitive claim details, or needs a case-specific recommendation. Escalation is also appropriate when financial impact is material and the request needs judgment beyond general KB guidance.
