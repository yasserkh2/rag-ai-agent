# Customer Care

## Service Overview
Customer Care covers general support intake, issue triage, status follow-up, and escalation handling for practices that need dependable communication with customers or internal stakeholders. It is suited to teams that want a more consistent first response, clearer issue ownership, and better handoff when problems cannot be resolved immediately.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Issue intake and case capture
- Status follow-up and communication support
- Routing to the right operational or specialist team
- Escalation handling for unresolved or urgent cases
- Customer-facing communication that keeps requests moving

## Common Practice Scenarios
- Requests are coming in through too many channels and updates are inconsistent.
- Customers are asking for status and the team lacks a clear follow-up rhythm.
- Leadership wants fewer dropped requests and better issue visibility.
- The organization needs stronger escalation discipline for urgent concerns.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice or customer name
- Contact details and preferred channel
- Short description of the issue
- Any known reference number, case number, or prior contact
- Business impact and urgency
- Requested next step, if known

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
Offer a concise explanation of customer care support, then determine whether the user needs issue intake, status help, or escalation. If the user is reporting a live problem, gather contact details and a short issue summary so the handoff is clean.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate when the user explicitly asks for a human, reports repeated failures, signals significant business impact, or raises privacy or compliance concerns. Urgent issues without a clear owner should also move quickly to human review.

## Example Customer Questions
- What kinds of issues does customer care handle?
- Can you help with status follow-up on an unresolved request?
- What details should we provide when opening an issue?
- When does customer care escalate to a human?
