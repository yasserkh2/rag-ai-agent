# Digital Marketing and Website Services

## Service Overview
Digital Marketing and Website Services supports healthcare practices that want stronger online presence, clearer messaging, and better digital growth workflows. It is typically relevant when a practice needs help with website quality, visibility, lead generation, or the coordination between digital presence and customer acquisition.

## What This Service Usually Includes
- Website improvement and content support
- Digital growth planning for healthcare practices
- Visibility and discoverability improvements
- Messaging alignment across online channels
- Review of current digital pain points and conversion blockers

## Common Practice Scenarios
- A practice website is outdated or difficult for prospective patients to use.
- Leadership wants stronger online visibility but lacks a focused plan.
- Marketing efforts are active but results are inconsistent.
- The team needs clearer messaging tied to service offerings and growth goals.

## Information To Collect During Intake
- Practice name and marketing contact
- Current website status or main digital channels
- Growth goals such as visibility, leads, or conversion improvement
- Known pain points with content, usability, or discoverability
- Priority services or specialties to highlight
- Preferred timeline and urgency

## How The Chatbot Should Respond
Explain the service in simple terms, then clarify whether the user is asking about website help, digital visibility, or broader marketing support. If the user wants next steps, capture goals and pain points rather than jumping straight into tactical promises.

## Escalation Guidance
Escalate when the user wants channel-specific strategy, budget-sensitive advice, or a custom review of their current website or campaigns. Human follow-up is also appropriate when the request needs tailored recommendations rather than general service information.

## Example Customer Questions
- What is included in digital marketing and website services?
- Can this help improve our online presence?
- What should we prepare before talking to your team?
- When should a marketing question be escalated?
